OUR COURSES

Courses We Offer

The college offers B.Tech courses in CE, EEE, ME, ECE, CSE, CSE (AI & ML), CSE (DS), CSE (IoT), IT, AIM and AID Branches. CE, EEE, ME, ECE and CSE course are NBA accredited.

Computers Science Engineering

Computer Science Engineering course encompasses the study of computer hardware and software. It is further sub divided into
AI & ML, CSO, CSD, IOT, IT.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) course is a branch of engineering that covers the study, design, development,
and application of electrical circuits, electronics, and communication systems.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction,
and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment.
